Our History

In late spring 2011, an old entertainment park
was put up for sale on the market for $14,500.
So my friends and I started saving up for it.
Once we had enough, we purchased the old place.
But since it had been poorly maintained,
it was in need of a total remodelling.
We started by fixing up the old go-kart track,
ordering new arcade machines, and finally,
installing three hi-speed waterslides.
Now it is the entertaintainment powerhouse
we always knew it had the potentential to be.
