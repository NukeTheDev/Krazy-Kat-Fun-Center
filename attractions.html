Attractions at Krazy Kat

Krazy Karts

Race your friends and feel
the adrenaline on our Krazy Karts


Krazy Kat has 2.5 miles of pure go-karting fun.
With sharp turns and bank corners designed
to test your tennacity and racing skill,
you will feel the wind rushing through
your hair as you cruise through our track.
Each ride lasts approximately 8 minutes.

Krazy Aqua-Park

Zip Down our Three
High Speed Waterslides.


The Titan is our slowest,
most family friendly waterslide.
Standing 19ft tall and reaching 4 mph.
It is the green slide shown in the photo.

The Monsoon is our second fastest slide
standing 30 ft tall and reaching 8 mph.
It is the blue slide shown in the photo.

The Megladon is our fastest
and tallest waterslide.
Standing 48ft tall and reaching 15mph.
It is the blue slide shown in the photo.
